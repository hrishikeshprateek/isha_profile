Usage tips for Instagram
- Profile/photo: use a circular crop with a thin ring in #F2A7A7 over #FAF0E6 background.
- Story highlights: use single-color icons on #FAF0E6 circular backgrounds; icon color #3B241A.
- Post templates: use the cream background (#FAF0E6) with a bold heading in #3B241A, accent details in #F2A7A7 and microcopy in #A68B7E.
- Gradient covers: use cyan -> purple (#00C4CC -> #7D2AE8) for promotional reels or travel teasers.

Accessibility / Contrast notes
- Primary text (#3B241A) on cream (#FAF0E6) has good contrast for headings.
- Accent #F2A7A7 on cream is decorative; avoid as main text color.
- For any CTA using #F2A7A7 on light backgrounds, ensure text on the CTA is dark (#3B241A) for readability.
